"Mr. D"

'D' is a figure briefly alluded to in internal McDonald's documents. Allegedly responsible for concealing any information related to Phil's legacy with McDonald's, 'D' was tasked with eradicating all evidence. Limited details are available about this individual, and their whereabouts remain elusive. Subsequently, 'D' was listed as a wanted individual on Interpol's website for undisclosed criminal activities.
